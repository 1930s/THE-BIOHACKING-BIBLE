==Sticky Film (TMPM) Dish Treatment Protocol== *Materials: **200 Proof Ethanol **H2O (MilliQ) **Glacial Acetic Acid **3-(trimethoxysilyl) propyl methacrylate, 98% *Procedure (To treat approximately 9 dishes): #Add 9.5 mL 200 Proof Ethanol to vial #Add 500 µL H2O (MilliQ) #Remove 50 µL solution - add 50 µL Glacial Acetic Acid #Remove 200 µL solution - add 200 µL 3-(trimethoxysilyl) propyl methacrylate #Add 1 mL to each dish - filter sterilized through a .22 µm syringe filter #Wash twice with MilliQ water. #Sterilize with 70% Ethanol, aspirate and let dry/sterilize under UV in TC hood
