==Superconcentration Experiments: Precip of Fb, PAGE of Eb, Fb, Gb== Fb was not folded at the same time as Eb and Gb b/c we ran out of p7308. The work done to Fb is intended to follow as closely as possible that which was done to Eb and Gb. ===PEG Precipitation of Fb=== * 12% PEG, 5M NaCl 
 Mix:
  120uL rxn (3 rxns)
  240uL 20% PEG -> 12% PEG
  40uL 5M NaCl -> 0.5M NaCl
  -------------
  400uL total reaction

 Ice 15 minutes
 
 Centrifuge @ 16G for 10 min
 
 Separate supernatant and pellet
   Reconstituted pellet in 16uL H2O

   Speedvaced supernatant in futile hope of reducing it to 60uL, erroneously thinking each agarose well holds 20uL (each holds 40uL)
       Reduced it to approximately 200uL, at which point it had thickened to a gel
       Reconsistuted with H2O to approximately 300uL

 Ran on 2% agarose gel, 10mM MgCl2 made by Matt two days ago
