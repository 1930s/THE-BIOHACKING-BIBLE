==Kinasing the 5' end of bought RNA oligomers ==
1µl 10X kinase buffer
5µl water
1µl Rnase inhibitor
1µl 2µM RNA (fc=.2µM or 2pmoles total)
1µl T4 kinase enzyme
1µl gamma-ATP (Perkin-Elmer(NEN) BLU-502z)

37°C for 1 hr.

clean with BioRad microspin columns

Run RNA on denaturing gel (see Acrylamide gels:[[Berglund:Acrylamide]])
