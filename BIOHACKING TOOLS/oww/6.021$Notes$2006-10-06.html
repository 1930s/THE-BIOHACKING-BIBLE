==Technical Writing== * Clear simple prose not laden with jargon * lab report is very different from research article * A lab report (e.g. high school) is a canned experiment written for the teacher who knows the results * A research article is written for peers and the results aren't known beforehand * This class, you're writing for peers a research article * Formulate figures first ** Start from results ** What's important about it? ** 3 bullet points per figure
