THE BIOBRICKS FOUNDATION TECHNICAL & LEGAL STANDARDS WORKSHOP

On November 5 and 6 on the campus of MIT, the BioBricks Foundation will host creators, users, and enablers of BioBrick standard biological parts in a workshop aimed at: * developing technical standards for the operation, characterization, and use of BioBrick™ parts and * developing legal strategies that enable the BioBrick™ parts collection to grow and remain freely available

DATE:
November 5-6, 2007

LOCATION:
MIT
Tang Center/Wong Auditorium
70 Memorial Drive
Cambridge, Massachusetts USA
(For a map of the Tang Center, click HERE )
