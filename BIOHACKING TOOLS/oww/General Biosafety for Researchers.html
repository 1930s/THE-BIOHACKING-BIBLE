==General Biosafety for Researchers== This is a classroom based training, that will serve as a general introduction to working in a BL1 lab. The course is approximately 45 minutes and is taught approximately once a month, so please do not wait to schedule this training. Note that to register for this class you must have an [[MIT Certificate]] and have previously completed the [[Training Needs Assessment]].
