[[CASTDan:Grants3 | Detail Description]]
Development of fluorescent alcohol sensor for food industry, fuel cells and/or gas/ethanol mixtures. The sensor is based on immobilization of Nile blue in PEG hydrogel. The resulting sensor changes both absorption (goes from blue to red) and fluorescence (shift toward blue) when exposed to various alcohol concentrations. The sensor will be used to monitor alcohol fermentations. '''4. Engineered Proteins for Low-Cost Metabolic Monitoring'''
