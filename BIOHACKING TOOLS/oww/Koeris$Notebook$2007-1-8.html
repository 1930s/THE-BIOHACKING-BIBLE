=Chemically competent bacterial cells= All cultures are grown at 37 deg C and 300 rpm shaking. The OD600 of the culture should be between 0.4 - 0.6. The culture is then centrifuged down at 3000rpm for 15min and the pellet resuspended in 1/10 volume of TSS. All steps are to be performed on ice. The tubes for the aliquots are also to be pre-chilled. ==Reagents needed== The following reagents are needed for the protocol. ===TSS (Transformation and storage solution)=== For 50ml use the following ratios: *LB: 30ml *PEG: 5.0g *DMSO: 2.5ml *MgCl2[1M]: 0.25ml *MgSO4[1M]: 0.5ml *100% glycerol: 7.5ml Store all competent cells at -80 deg C for future use.
