'''BASIC WESTERN BLOT PROTOCOL''' • Block membrane 1 hour in blocking buffer (5% carnation non-fat dry milk dissolved in water.) • Incubate membrane for 1 hour in blocking buffer containing primary antibody. • Wash the membrane 2X5 min, 2X10 min in 0.05% Tween 20 in TBS. • Incubate membrane for 30min in blocking buffer containing secondary antibody with desired (HRP, AP, etc.) conjugate. • Wash the membrane 2X5 min, 2X10 min in 0.05% Tween 20 in TBS. • Use ECL according to manufacturer to develop.
