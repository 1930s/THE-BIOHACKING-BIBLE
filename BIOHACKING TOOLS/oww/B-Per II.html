==Purpose== B-Per and B-Per II are reagents for bacterial lysis. It is a gentle reagent that can be used to lyse cells without denaturing proteins. It has been used to lyse ''E. coli'' cells to measure the GFP concentration via the [[Endy:Victor3 plate reader|Endy lab plate reader]]. ==Procurement== B-Per and B-Per II are sold by Pierce ([http://www.piercenet.com). ==Use== For a usage protocol developed by [[Ania Labno]] and [[Barry Canton]], see this [[Endy:Victor3 plate reader/Calibrating the GFP-separated label|page]]. ==Safety== B-Per is an irritant. ==References== *[http://www.piercenet.com/poppers/nonanimated/bper/index.html Product webpage] *[http://www.piercenet.com/resources/browse.cfm?fldID=1D083447-2515-49E1-BFD7-D2A7341B899A Product FAQ] ==Contact== *[[Barry Canton]] *[[Ania Labno]]
