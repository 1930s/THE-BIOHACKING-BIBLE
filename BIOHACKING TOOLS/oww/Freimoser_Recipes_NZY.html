{{Template:FreimoserTopTemplate2}} ==NZY medium== ====Composition (for 1L)==== * 10g Yeast extract * 5g NaCl * 2g Glucose * 16g NZ-Amine A Fill up to 1 liter and adjust to pH 7. ====Comment==== * We use this medium to grow bacteria if purification on amylose resin (with MBP tag) is sought * The use of NZ-amine A also lactose free (in contrast to bacteriological peptone) * NZ-amine A is available from Sigma (C0626-250g)
