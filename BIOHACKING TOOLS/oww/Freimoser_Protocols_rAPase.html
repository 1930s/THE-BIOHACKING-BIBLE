{{Template:FreimoserTopTemplate2}} __NOTOC__ ===Acid phosphatase (rAPase) assay=== ==rAPase assay== This protocol follows the description of [http://www.genetics.org/cgi/content/full/169/4/1859| Huang & O'Shea 2005 (Genetics 169 (4): 1859-1871)]. # take 50 μl from a yeast culture # add 200 μl nitrophenyl-phosphate (20 mM)(in 100 mM NaAcetate, pH 4.2) # incubate 15 min at 28°C (in strains with high rAPase activity the incubation time must be reduced) # add 200 μl ice cold TCA (10 %) # add 400 μl Na2CO3 (2 M) # spin down, 2 min, max speed # measure absorbance at 420 nm # normalize OD420 with cell density (OD600), reaction time (15 min) and culture volume used for assay (50 μl). Miller units = (OD420x1000)/(OD600x15x50) ==Solutions== * nitrophenyl-phosphate-solution: ** nitrophenyl-phosphate (MW = 371.12 g/mol) ** make 20 mM solution in NaAcetate (100 mM, pH 4.2) (0.00742 g/ml) * 10% TCA (trichloroacetic acid) * 2 M Na2CO3 (sodium carbonate)
