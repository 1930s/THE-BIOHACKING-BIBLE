=CellDesigner: Reactions= ===Overview=== {| |- | valign=top| [[Image:CellDesigner GUI TopMenu ReactionSymbols.png|400px|CellDesigner Top Menu]] * '''Reactions''' represents '''state transition''' of the connected Species, for example, biochemical reactions, interaction between proteins, and regulatory relations between genes. * Each biochemical reaction as a '''specific symbol'''(see figure). | [[Image:CellDesigner-Reactions.png|300px|CellDesigner Reaction Symbols]] |} ===Functions related to compounds=== * '''Create''' a new reaction: Select the approriate transition icon. Select 'source' compound, and then select 'Destination' compound. A transition is created. * '''Delete''' a reaction: Select the reaction. Menu: Edit --> Delete. * '''Edit''' a reaction : Double click a Reaction, click and change anything in the dialog box, and then, see what is changed after click "Apply" button. ===Resources=== * [http://www.systems-biology.org/cd/help/CDH_Reaction-1.html CellDesigner Reactions tutorial]
