__NOTOC__ =Central Limit Theorem= ==Experimentalist point of view== ===Assume there is some "parent distribution" for your possible experimental measurements=== ===We want to estimate the mean of this parent distribution (why?)=== ===[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Law_of_large_numbers#A_weaker_law_and_proof Law of large numbers]=== ===If we take a few measurements and average them: ''the average is the best guess for the mean of the parent population'' AND ''if we repeat this many times, our averages will have a normal distribution about the population mean''=== ==[http://elonen.iki.fi/articles/centrallimit/index.en.html#demo A pretty good experimental demo]== ==Some other links== ===http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illustration_of_the_central_limit_theorem=== ===downloadable simulation: http://www.vias.org/simulations/simusoft_cenlimit.html=== ===Java dice rolling: http://www.stat.sc.edu/~west/javahtml/CLT.html=== ===Pretty good graphical demonstration: [http://www.statisticalengineering.com/central_limit_theorem.htm CLT]===
