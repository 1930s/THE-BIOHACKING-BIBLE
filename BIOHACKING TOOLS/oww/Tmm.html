This presentation from the Bio[[MIBLab]] [[Summer 2006 Workshop]] is a [[Summer 2006 Workshop#Analysis|analysis]] bioinformatics tool. The goal of Tmm is to mine public microarray datasets for reliably and reproducibly coexpressed genes. In this manner, a user can obtain lists of usually 10-100 transcripts that coexpress with the user's gene of interest. ==Synopsis== Analysis of multiple microarray data sets to find reliably coexpressed genes ==Features== *Useful to make a small list of probable targets. *Useful for some validation? ::Similar to GOMiner validation. ::Speed will inhibit this. *Semantics is a barrier to usefulness. ==Full Presentation== [[Media:Tmm.ppt|Tmm PowerPoint Presentation]] ==Links== [http://microarray.cpmc.columbia.edu/tmm/index.html Tmm Homepage]
