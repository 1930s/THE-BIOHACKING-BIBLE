Ethanol Seed Sterilization

---- #Tap seeds into 1.5mL tubes (labeled with VWR pen) #Add 1mL 70% Ethanol, 0.1% Triton solution to each tube #Vortex at 8 for 5 minutes #Aspirate liquid off of seeds #Add about 1mL 70% Ethanol to each tube #In sterile hood, pipette seeds onto filter paper (labeled with pencil) #Let Ethanol evaporate away from filter paper #When filter paper is dry, tap seeds onto plates #Tape plates, wrap in foil, and refrigerate at 4 degrees for 3-4 days before unwrapping and placing plates in growth chamber |}
