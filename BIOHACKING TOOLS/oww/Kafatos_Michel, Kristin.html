. == Research Interests ==
My research interests are directed primarily toward the role of the innate immune system in the insect vector during host-parasite interactions in a variety of interrelated areas:
*Regulation of humoral innate immune responses in the insect vector by serine protease inhibitors *The role of immune responsive organs, especially the insect blood cells (hemocytes), in the recognition, modulation and initiation of effector systems *The interplay of immune responsive organs (midgut, fat body, and hemocytes) and the potential role of cytokines in this interplay

Other areas of interest include:
*Hematopoiesis in insects *Evolution of immunity genes
