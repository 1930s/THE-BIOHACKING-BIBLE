[[Image:Laura_Sontag.JPG|150px|right]] '''Laura Sontag''' (Computational and Systems Biology doctoral student) Advisors: Professors Peter Sorger (Biology/BE, MIT) and Douglas Lauffenburger (BE/ChemE/Biology, MIT), in collaboration with Professor Tyler Jacks (Biology/Center for Cancer Research, MIT) Computational and experimental analysis of mitogenic signaling pathways activated by oncogenic Ras. The Ras signaling cascade constitutes a major pathway shown to be deregulated due to activating mutations in 30% of all human tumors. The vast majority of mutations occur in the K-ras gene. We use genetically engineered mice that harbor a conditional, activatable K-ras allele as a model of human cancer.
