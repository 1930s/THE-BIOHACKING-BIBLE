The BioBricks Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. We rely on support from individuals like you to carry out our worldwide mission to promote and protect the open development, sharing, and free reuse of BioBrick standard biological parts. All contributions to the BioBricks Foundation are U.S. tax deductible.

Support the BBF today by becoming a member, volunteering, or making a donation.

Most of all: Share Your Parts!

Gifts in Honor or Memorial
A contribution to the BioBricks Foundation in honor or in memory of someone brings special meaning to your gift. We will send notification of your honor or memorial gift to those you designate.
To make a gift in someone's honor, please include the name and address of that person.
To make a gift in someone's memory, please include the name and address of any individual(s), friends, family members or colleagues you would like us to notify.

Gifts of Stock and Securities
The BioBricks Foundation welcomes gifts of stock and securities, which may also help a donor avoid capital gains tax on appreciated assets. All such gifts are U.S. tax deductible to the full extent provided by the law. In most cases, gifts of stock or securities can be completed through a simple transfer of assets between financial services firms.
Please contact Lauren Ha (lauren AT biobricks DOT org) for instructions and account information.
