Currently, The BioBricks Foundation's goals are: *to develop and implement legal strategies to ensure that BioBrick™ standard biological parts remain freely available to the public; *to encourage the development of codes of standard practice for the use of BioBrick™ standard biological parts; and *to develop and provide educational and scientific materials to allow the public to use and improve existing BioBrick™ standard biological parts, and contribute new BioBrick™ standard biological parts.
