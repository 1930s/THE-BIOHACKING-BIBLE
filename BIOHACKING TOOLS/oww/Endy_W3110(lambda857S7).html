Back to [[Endy:E._coli_Strains| Endy lab E coli strains]] * '''Strain:''' W3110(λ857S7) * '''Genotype:''' See [http://cgsc.biology.yale.edu:80/cgi-bin/sybgw/cgsc/Strain/5446 CGSC#4474] for the genotype of W3110 * '''Phenotype/Notes:''' This strain harbors a lambda prophage, with the cI857 mutation, and an amber mutation in the ''S'' gene. * '''Date:''' 12/2005 * '''Experimenter:''' [[User:Stpierre|Francois St-Pierre]] * '''Strain obtained from:''' Generous gift from Prof. Amos Oppenheim. * '''Stock preparation:''' The strain was received as an agar plate. The plate was incubated at 30C until colonies where clearly visible. A single colony was grown in LB at 30C to OD600 = 2.4. Glycerol stocks were made by mixing 1ml of culture with 1ml of 40% glycerol, vortexing, and storing at -85C. * '''Physical location of the stock:''' ** Lab stocks: none ** [[User:Stpierre|Francois St-Pierre]]'s stocks: 2 vials in box "Francois's Ecoli 1" (as of 12/2005). #ID = FSP B8
