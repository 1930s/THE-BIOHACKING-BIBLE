== Redigest of Kai\X-P == Since our gel failed and we lost all of our digest product, we redigested KaiA/B/C with X-P, following the same reactions as [[IGEM:Harvard/2006/Cyanobacteria/Notebook/2006-8-11#Digestion_of_GeneART_plasmids|yesterday]]. * 4 uL DNA (~800 µg DNA) * 17.25 uL H2O * 2.5 uL buffer (Buffer 3) * 0.5 uL enzyme 1 (XbaI) * 0.5 uL enzyme 2 (PstI) * 0.25 uL BSA The digest ran for 2 hours at 40C. == CIP treatment of pSB4A3\S-P, J04500\S-P, and pSB1A2\X-P == Yesterday we gel-purified three digested backbones: pSB4A3\S-P, J00450\S-P, and pSB1A2\X-P. Normally one does the CIP treatment immediately after the digest instead of gel-purifying first. Since we didn't add CIP then, we had to add it now. We didn't want to do another gel purification after this step because our yield would likely be very low (the backbones have around 3 ng/µL concentration of DNA). Therefore we had to inactivate the CIP by heat. * Add 0.1 µL CIP to each digest product * Incubate at 37C for 1 h * Add EDTA to 5 mM * Heat shock at 75C for 15 min ==KaiA/B/C\X-P digest gel == [[Image: 2006-08-12 Cyano Digest Gel 2.jpg|thumb|left]] [[Image: 2006-08-12 Cyano Digest Gel 2 mod.jpg|thumb|left]] [[Image: 2006-08-12 cyano postcut.jpg|thumb|left]]
