=Development and analysis of kinetic models of intracellular networks= '''Author(s): '''Frank Bruggeman
'''Affiliations:''' Manchester Interdisciplinary Biocentre
'''Contact:''' frank.bruggeman@manchester.ac.uk
'''Keywords:''' kinetic models; tutorial ==Abstract== ===Proposed Content=== In this workshop, I will give an introduction to the construction and analysis of kinetic models. Mass-action kinetic and enzyme kinetics will be briefly discussed. A number of kinetic models dealing with metabolism, signal transduction and gene expression will be introduced and discussed. Simple principles that govern the control and robustness of networks will be illustrated.
