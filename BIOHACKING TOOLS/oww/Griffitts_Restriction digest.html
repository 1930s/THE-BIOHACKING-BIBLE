{{Griffitts}} Restriction Digest ==Materials== * ddH20 * DNA sample on ice * Appropriate 10X buffer (see below) on ice * 10X BSA on ice (if necessary; see below) * Restriction enzyme(s) (see below) on ice * CIP (keep in freezer until needed) ==Recipes== ===15 μL reaction=== * 5 μL DNA * 1.8 μL appropriate 10X buffer * 1.5 μL 10X BSA (if needed--see below) * Restriction enzyme(s) (see specific enzymes below for exact amounts) ** Don't immerse the tip, draw from the surface to avoid excess enzyme * Add ddH20 to bring the final volume to 15 μL Note: Use this recipe when verifying plasmid inserts
Note: You can check this reaction after 1 hour ===30 μL reaction=== * 12 μL DNA * 3.3 μL appropriate 10X buffer * 3.0 μL 10X BSA (if needed--see below) * Restriction enzyme(s) (see specific enzymes below for exact amounts) ** Don't immerse the tip, draw from the surface to avoid excess enzyme * Add ddH20 to bring the final volume to 30 μL Note: Use this recipe when your DNA concentrations are sufficient ===40 μL reaction=== * 20 μL DNA * 4.3 μL appropriate 10X buffer * 4.0 μL 10X BSA (if needed--see below) * Restriction enzyme(s) (see specific enzymes below for exact amounts) ** Don't immerse the tip, draw from the surface to avoid excess enzyme * Add ddH20 to bring the final volume to 40 μL Note: Use this recipe when your DNA concentrations are low ==Procedure== * Combine ingredients for recipe * Incubate at 37°C for 2.5 to 8 hours * If preparing a ligation, add 0.5 μL CIP to the vector 30 minutes prior to ending the reaction * When the reaction is complete you can store your samples in the -20°C freezer or proceed to [[Griffitts:In-gel ligation|in-gel ligation]] ==Enzymes== {| border="1" cellpadding="5" cellspacing="0" class="wikitable sortable" |+'''Restriction Enzymes''' |- !
