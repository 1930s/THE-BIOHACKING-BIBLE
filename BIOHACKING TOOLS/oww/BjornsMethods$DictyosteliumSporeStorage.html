==Long-Term Storage of Dictyo Spores== #Take a plate of Dictyo that has turned into spores and slam it on the counter to force all spore heads to the lid of the plate. #Add 0.6 mL SM media to lid of plate and use pipet to mix spores with media #Add spore mixture to 2 mL tube with 0.6 mL 40% glycerol #Put in -85 C freezer for storage up to a decade
