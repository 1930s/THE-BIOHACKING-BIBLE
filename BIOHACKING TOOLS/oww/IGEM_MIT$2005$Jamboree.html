The end-of-competition '''JAMBOREE''' will take place during the first weekend of November here in Cambridge, MA. The exact schedule is still TBD but plan to reserve the entire weekend of November 5-6, 2005. In order to present our system at the Jamboree we will need to meet two submission requirements: *All parts used in our system entered into the Registry and documented such that somebody else could re-use the part later. *A web-page summary and description of the system. See the IAP 2004 project pages for some first-generation examples.
