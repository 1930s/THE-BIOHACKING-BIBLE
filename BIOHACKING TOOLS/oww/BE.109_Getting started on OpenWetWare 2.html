__TOC__ ==Starting your user page== *Perhaps your user page is the most comfortable place for you to start editing. A brief description of who you are will introduce you to your classmates and the rest of the OpenWetWare community. [[Image:Edit User Page.jpg|frame|center|Click on your '''username''' to edit]] *To access your user page, log in, then click on your '''username''' at the top right of any OpenWetWare page. Then, click on the [[Image:EditButton.jpg]] tab.
