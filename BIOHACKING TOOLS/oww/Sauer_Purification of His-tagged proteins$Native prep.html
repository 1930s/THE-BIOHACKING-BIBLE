=Introduction= This is a general protocol for purification of His6-tagged proteins under native conditions. Note that you may need to modify this protocol to fit your specific needs. This protocol is a modified version of that provided with Ni2+-NTA resin from [http://www1.qiagen.com/SelectCountry.aspx Qiagen]. =Buffers= ===Lysis buffer=== 50 mM NaH2PO4 500 mM NaCl 10 mM imidazole Adjust to pH 8 ===Wash buffer === 50 mM NaH2PO4 500 mM NaCl 20 mM imidazole Adjust to pH 8 ===Elution buffer=== 50 mM NaH2PO4 500 mM NaCl 250 mM imidazole Adjust to pH 8
