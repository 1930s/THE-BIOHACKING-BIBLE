Copy the below, '''replace Calendar: with your calendar name''' in all of the following calendars that you want displayed. Note: replace the angle brackets also (they are there to force you to change the name). For example, a public calendar could be named Calendar:MyCalendar or a lab calendar may be MyLab:Calendar. __NOTOC__

view=days
days=1
date=yesterday
format=%name/%year-%m-%d
formattitle="%M %day, %year (%l) (Yesterday)"
skipempty=1
weekformat=list
