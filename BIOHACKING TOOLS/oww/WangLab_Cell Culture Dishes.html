==Where to order== The dishes are made by Corning Inc. There are three types of cell culture dishes: * small (35 mmx10 mm, #430165), * medium (60 mmx15 mm, #430166), * large (100 mmx20 mm, #430167) ==Description== The amount of cell culture medium can be calculated from the size of the dish. We put about * 2 ml medium into the small dish, * 4-5 ml medium into the media dish, * 12 ml medium into the large dish. [[Category:WangLab_Page]]
