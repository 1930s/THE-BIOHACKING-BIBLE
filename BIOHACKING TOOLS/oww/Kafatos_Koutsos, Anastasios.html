== Research Interests ==
My research interests were directed towards functional genomics in the mosquito, malaria vector, Anopheles gambiae. My work in the lab included the following :
*Functional characterisation of LRIM1 in Anopheles gambiae. *Microarray transcriptomic analyses in the mosquito, malaria vector, Anopheles gambiae. *The initial phase of building AnoEST, a database of expressed sequences of Anopheles gambiae
