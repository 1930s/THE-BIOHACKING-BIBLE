==Poisson Distribution== :p(k;\lambda)=\frac{\lambda^k e^{-\lambda}}{k!},\,\! ===Is the limit of the bionomial distribution when probability of success goes to zero, number of trials goes to infinity, and p*n = lambda=== \ \sigma_{k}\, =\, \sqrt{\lambda} ===For a given collection of data, thought to be Poisson distributed, the maximum likelihood fit is=== :\lambda = \frac {\sum{x_i}}{N}, ===where x_i are the counts recorded in each trial, and N is the number of trials=== ===Example: decay of radioactive sample=== : p_\mathrm{Poisson}(k;\lambda) \approx p_\mathrm{normal}(k;\mu=\lambda,\sigma^2=\lambda)\,
