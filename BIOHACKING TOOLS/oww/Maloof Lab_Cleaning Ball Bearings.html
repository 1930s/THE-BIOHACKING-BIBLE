Cleaning Ball Bearings

---- #Place used ball bearing in a 1L plastic beaker. Use screen over beaker top to retain bearings while rinsing. #Wash with several changes of detergent and water, with vigorous swirling until they appear clean. #Rinse 3X (or more) with water, until no longer soapy and water does not foam #Incubate in 5% bleach for 30 minutes #Rinse 3X (or more) with water, until bleach smell is gone #Rinse 1X with 95% EtOH, to dry #Autoclave, 20 minutes, dry cycle #If any moisture remains, place on paper towel to dry |}
