__TOC__ ==Characterisation==
This is a prioritised list of devices utilising the Hrp system that we would like to characterise.
===[[IGEM:IMPERIAL/2007/Projects/Hrp_System/Systems/Hrp_Device 1 | Device 1: One input]]=== [[image:ICGEMS_dvc_HrpR&S+L.png|right|thumb|350px|Follower: [[IGEM:IMPERIAL/2007/Projects/Hrp_System/Systems/HRP1 |pHrpL promoter - RS (single operon) only]]]]
This device is a pHrpL promoter utilising a single RS operon only.
A single promoter activates HrpR and HrpS expression simultaneously.
===[[IGEM:IMPERIAL/2007/Projects/Hrp_System/Systems/Hrp_Device 2 |Device 2: Two inputs]]=== [[image:ICGEMS_dvc_HrpR&S+V+L.png|right|thumb|350px|AND with one inversion: pHrpL promoter - RS and V]]

This device simulates a basic AND gate with one inverted input.
Again, HrpR and HrpS are controlled by a single promoter. HrpV expression is
dependent on a different promoter. The '''R and S proteins activate''' the pHrpL
promoter while the '''V protein represses''' it.
