THE BIOBRICKS FOUNDATION TECHNICAL & LEGAL STANDARDS WORKSHOP 2

On March 1, 2008 on the Mission Bay campus of UCSF, the BioBricks Foundation will host creators, users, and enablers of BioBrick standard biological parts in its 2nd Technical & Legal Standards Workshop. The goals of these series of Workshops are: * develop technical standards for the operation, characterization, and use of BioBrick™ parts and * develop legal strategies that enable the BioBrick™ parts collection to grow and remain freely available
