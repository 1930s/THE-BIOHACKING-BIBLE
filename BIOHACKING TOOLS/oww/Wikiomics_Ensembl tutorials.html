Click on '''Filters''' * in the central panel click on '''+''' next to '''Gene''' * check a box next to '''ID list limit''' * from pull down menu right to '''ID list limit''' select type of your IDs, i.e. Unigene ID(s) * either cut and paste your IDs (try four UniGene entries below) in a form or select text file with your IDs, one ID per line.
