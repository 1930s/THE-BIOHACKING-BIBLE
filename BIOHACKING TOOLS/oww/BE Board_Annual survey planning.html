==2006 Survey Planning== Please edit the [[BE Board: 2006 Annual Survey Questions|2006 Draft Survey]], which has color-coded changes from the 2005 version. For further reference: [[BE Board: 2005 Annual Survey Questions|2005 Survey Questions]] The annual survey is a BE board initiative to ascertain student opinions, concerns and interests. The information is used to plan social and academic events, compile the overall opinion of the students when the department or the visiting committee requests feedback, and as a a long term resource to see how the department improves and changes over time. As such, the survey normally undergoes an annual update to include questions on pertinent issues and remove other items which may have become irrelevant.
