==The Jamboree Presentation, 4-5 November 2006== The Jamboree was held in the Stata Center and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology from 4-5 November 2006 and allowed all the iGEM teams from around the world to present their research and ideas to the entire synthetic biology community. For more information, please visit the [http://www.igem2006.com iGEM 2006 website].
