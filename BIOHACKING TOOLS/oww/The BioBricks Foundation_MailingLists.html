The BBF maintains four email-based discussion lists. If you are interested in discussing or helping with a topic, please subscribe yourself. If you can't stand the discussion, you can also unsubscribe yourself. The four discussion lists are: *Volunteer -- Help with the workings, strategy, and leadership of the BBF. **Sign up for [http://biobricks.org/mailman/listinfo/volunteers_biobricks.org Volunteers] now! *Technical Standards -- Share and discuss details of the open technical standards setting process that defines BioBrick standard biological parts. **Sign up for [http://biobricks.org/mailman/listinfo/standards_biobricks.org Standards] now! *Legal -- Develop and discuss the legal scheme used to keep BioBricks parts free to study, use, and modify. **Sign up for [http://biobricks.org/mailman/listinfo/legal_biobricks.org Legal] now! *SB4.0 -- Help organize and participate in SB4.0, the Fourth International Meeting on Synthetic Biology. **Sign up for [http://biobricks.org/mailman/listinfo/sb4.0_biobricks.org SB4.0] now!
