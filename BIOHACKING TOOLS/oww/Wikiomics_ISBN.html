An [[wikipedia:ISBN|ISBN]] (International Standard Book Number) is a unique identifier which is associated with most commercial books. It is usually found on the back cover. This wiki lets you cite a book by giving only its ISBN. The corresponding data will be automatically retrieved and formatted by [[Biblio|the system]].
