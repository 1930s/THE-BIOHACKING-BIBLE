==Quantomix Wet EM== [http://www.quantomix.com Quantomix] is a company which makes sealable electron transparent capsules for Wet EM. I have used these capsules to do some EM on IC21 cells. I tried uranyl acetate and osmium tetraoxide - standard stains. I have also tried collodial gold-streptavidin-biotin-phalloidin - to try to label actin specifically. But most interesting of all - I tried using quantum dots-streptavidin-biotin-phalloidin - to label actin. This allows us to do fluorescent microscopy and electron microscopy on the exact same sample, without further modification. ---- Back to [[User:Timp0 | Winston]]
