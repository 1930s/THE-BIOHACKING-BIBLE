Simon comes from Auckland, New Zealand. He did his PhD in [http://biochemistry.ucsf.edu/~blackburn/ Elizabeth Blackburn's] lab at UCSF, studying telomeres and telomerase using ''Saccharomyces cerevisiae''. His postdoc research with [http://www.mcdb.ucla.edu/Research/Jacobsen/LabWebSite/index.html Steve Jacobsen] at UCLA focussed on mechanisms of gene silencing in ''Arabidopsis''.
