==Excitation of Neon== ===Purpose/Procedure=== The purpose of this lab is to discover the quantized excitations of neon gas. To discover this, we scatter electrons off the neon atoms and see what energy of the electrons excites the atoms to their next level. For more details see [http://openwetware.org/wiki/Physics307L:People/Le/Notebook/071119 my lab book entry] ===Data=== Graphs of accelerating voltage versus the current in the probe http://openwetware.org/images/1/14/Neongraph3.JPG http://openwetware.org/images/3/31/Neongraph4.JPG ===Results=== Peaks found at voltages:16.25, 16.75, 18.25, 20.50 According to a paper included in [http://www-hep.phys.unm.edu/%7Egold/phys307L/manual.pdf last year's lab manual (see page 91)], the resonance voltages for Ne are: 16.7, 18.65, 19.75, 20.10. Our values are very very close to these reported values. For a quantitative look at the success of this experiment, let us compare the 2 values.
