O'Neill Lab]]''' University of Connecticut Department of Molecular and Cell Biology [[Oneill_Lab:Protocols|Back to O'Neill Lab Protocols]] ==Scott-Simanis Protocol== For making chemically competent ''E. coli'' ===Materials=== *[[Oneill_Lab:Media#ψb|ψb]] *ψa plates **ψa is made by adding agar to ψb liquid media to a concentration of 1.5% (w/v) and autoclaving *[[Oneill_Lab:Media#TFBI|TFBI]] *[[Oneill_Lab:Media#TFBII|TFBII]] ===Procedure=== #Streak frozen stock of cells on ψa plate #Incubate overnight at 37°C #Pick a single colony and inoculate 5ml of ψb #Incubate at 37°C overnight #Subculture 5ml into 100ml of ψb #Grow for about 2 hours, until OD600 = 0.5 #Chill cells on ice for 5 minutes #Pellet cells at 4000 rpm for 5 minutes at 4°C #Decant media and resuspend cells in 2/5 volume of TFBI #Chill cells on ice for 5 minutes #Pellet cells at 4000 rpm for 5 minutes at 4°C #Decant media and resuspend cells in 1/25 volume of TFBII #Aliquot cells and snap freeze #*Store at -80°C ==Links and Contacts== [[User:Seth|Seth Kasowitz]] [mailto:seth.kasowitz@uconn.edu] [http://www.oneill.mcb.uconn.edu/Michael/MONEILLhome.html|O'Neill Lab Homepage]
