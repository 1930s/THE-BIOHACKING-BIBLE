==Cytoplasmic nucleotide levels== It is hard to measure the cytoplasmic levels of free NTPs. Several groups have tried to make these measurements when studying the growth rate dependence of rRNA synthesis Petersen Gaal. Both of these groups calculated nucleotide levels per unit absorbance. Liang et al.Liang used this data to estimate that cytoplasmic ATP levels are in the range of 10mM. That corresponds to ~1e7 of each nucleotide per cell.
