Rich Media for Caulobacter crescentus ==Ingredients== *2.0g bactopeptone *1.0g yeast extract *0.3g MgSO4 **From Sigma *10mL 50 mM CaCl2 **From Emanuele: not necessary for Caulobacter growth at MIT (perhaps enough calcium already in yeast extract or kitchen flasks). **Now we use 1mL of 0.5M CaCl2 from the MIT Kitchen --[[Sri Kosuri]] ([[User talk:Skosuri|talk]]) 16:49, 19 April 2007 (EDT) *1L H2O (milliQ or distilled) ==Instructions== *Add ingredients and autoclave (250°F, 22psi, 30-60min depending on volume). *For plates, usually add 1.5% (w/v) BactoAgar as well. ==Notes== *If PYE is to be aliquoted, first prepare 1L as described, then aliquot/pour into 100mL bottles. Finally, reautoclave individual bottles
