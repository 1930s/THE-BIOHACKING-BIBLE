OpenWetWare is an effort to promote the sharing of information, know-how, and wisdom among researchers and groups who are working in biology & biological engineering. Learn more about us [http://openwetware.org/wiki/OpenWetWare:About here]. If you would like edit access, would be interested in helping out, or want your lab website hosted on OpenWetWare, please let [http://openwetware.org/wiki/OpenWetWare:How_to_join us know].
