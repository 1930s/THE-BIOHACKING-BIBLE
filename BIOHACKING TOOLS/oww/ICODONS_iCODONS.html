---- =About iCODONS= iCODONS is a independent collaborative research group working on Computational Sciences and Discovery Informatics. iCODONS is working to develop algorithms and software for Life Sciences Informatics. Application of Visual Analytics and Scientific Workflow in Life Sciences Informatics is one of our major research concentration. iCODONS is also working in area of Mathematical Modelling and Computer Simulation of different biological and chemical phenomenon. Currently we are working on Biological Interaction Network modelling, Viral dynamics modelling, Physiological events modelling and ADME-Tox prediction & modelling. To know more about our work please go through Research & Project Section of this wiki.
