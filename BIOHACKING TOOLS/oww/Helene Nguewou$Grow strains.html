==Materials== *Agar plate with the appropriate antibacterial resistance *disposable scoop *bacteria to grow ==Procedure== *Label the plate with your initials, content and date. *use a disposable scoop to scrape some of the bacteria from the culture tube. *Disperse it on the bottom agar plate. *Put the plate overnight in the incubator (~37 °C). *The next morning seal the plate with parafilm and store it at 4 °C max 3 weeks.
