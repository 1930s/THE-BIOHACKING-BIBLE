=Current methods of insulin administration=


==Syringe / Prefilled insulin pens== [[Image:IC2007 InsuCurMeth1.jpg|right|200px]] *Continuous administration required *Manual calculation of dosage *Need to carry vials and needles




==Insulin pumps== [[Image:IC2007 InsuCurMeth2.jpg|right|200px]] *Needs battery for operation *Need to manually adjust level when exercising *Soft needle inserted under skin – replace every 2 days




==Inhalers== [[Image:IC2007 InsuCurMeth3.jpg|right|200px]] *Currently under testing – No FDA approval *Still need to manually adjust dosage















=Advantages of Bac-to-Insulin= *'''Continuous administration'''
*'''Regulated levels of secretion'''
*'''Does not need frequent replacing'''
*'''Long lasting effects'''
