{{EndyLab}} {|cellspacing="5" cellpadding="10" style="background:#3674C2; width: 750px;" |-valign="top" |style="background:#ffffff"| ==Introduction== The Endy lab has a [http://las.perkinelmer.com/Catalog/default.htm?CategoryID=VICTOR%3Csup%3E3%3C%2Fsup%3E Perkin Elmer Victor3] plate reader with 2 dispenser pumps. If you are interested in trying our plate reader or finding out more about it, contact [[Barry Canton|Barry]]. The machine can make the following classes of measurement - *Time-resolved fluorometry *Fluorometry *[[Endy:Victor3 plate reader/Absorbance labels|Photometry]] *Luminometry *Fluorescence polarization *LANCE We have a variety of filters to be used with these measurements, they are described [[Endy:Victor3 plate reader/filters|here]]. If you haven't used the machine before, have a look at this [[Endy:Victor3 Getting Started|overview of using the plate reader]].
