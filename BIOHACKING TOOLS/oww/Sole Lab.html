{|cellspacing="5" cellpadding="10" style="background:#000; width: 750px;" |-valign="top" |style="background:#ffffff"| The [http://complex.upf.es Complex Systems Lab], located at the [http://www.prbb.es PRBB] (Barcelona Biomedical Research Park) within the Life Sciences Department of [http://www.upf.es Universitat Pompeu Fabra] and part of the [http://nemo.imim.es/grib/ GRIB], is an interdisciplinary research team (including biologists, physicists, computer sciencists and engineers) exploring the evolution of complex systems, searching for their common laws of organization.
