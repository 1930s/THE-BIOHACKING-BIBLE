CGSC#7740, Source person = M.S.Guyer (choice 1) * '''Stock preparation:''' The stock came as a few drops of a glycerolized culture placed on a filter disc. The filter disc was placed on a LB plate and ~200ul of LB was added on the filter to humidify it. A sterile loop (or toothpick), after touching the liquid, was used to streak on the same plate. The plate was incubated at 37C. A single colony was then grown in LB at 30C until it just reached late log phase - early saturation (OD600~2.3). Glycerol stocks were made by mixing 1ml of culture with 1ml of 40% glycerol, vortexing, and storing at -85C. * '''Physical location of the stock:''' ** [[User:Stpierre|Francois St-Pierre]]'s stocks: 4 vials in box "Francois's Ecoli 1" (as of Oct3,2005).
