We are a team of molecular biologists, biochemists, geneticists and biotechnologists engaged in research and development on human genetics and molecular biotechnology. We provide molecular diagnostic services for the Malta department of Health and other clients for the following hereditary conditions; Thalassaemia and other haemoglobinopathies Cystic Fibrosis Gangliosidosis Coagulopathies. others will be added in due course.
