=== Follow this protocol if the transfected cells are to be passed on fibronectin-coated dish one day before imaging.=== * Commercial fibronectin (bFn) has a concentration of 1mg/ml. They need to be diluted 50 times with water to 20ug/ml. * Put uncovered dish with fibronectin (150ul/dish) in incubator overnight. Evenly spread the liquid in the well using pipette tip. * During passing the cells (right after centrifugation step), pipette out the excess liquid. * Pass the cells to the fibronectin covered dishes. NOTE: The above protocol is for MEF cells.
