Welcome to the Howlett laboratory Wiki. The newly established Howlett Laboratory is located in the [http://www.uri.edu/cels/cmb/people/faculty/nhowlett/ Department of Cell and Molecular Biology] in the University of Rhode Island, Kingston, Rhode Island. We are interested in understanding the causes of the rare genetic disorder [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/dispomim.cgi?id=227650 Fanconi anemia (FA)]. FA is characterized by developmental defects, bone marrow failure, and an increased susceptibility to cancer. ='''Positions Available'''= Several positions are currently available in the Howlett lab.
