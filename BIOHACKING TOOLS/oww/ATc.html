anhydrotetracycline (ATc) is a tetracycline analog, showing increased (30x) affinity for tet repressor. 100 ng/mL gives induction at half maximum. ==References== [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/utils/lofref.fcgi?PrId=3001&uid=8652521&db=pubmed&url=http://dx.doi.org/10.1021/bi952683e Tetracycline analogs affecting binding to Tn10-Encoded Tet repressor trigger the same mechanism of induction.]
