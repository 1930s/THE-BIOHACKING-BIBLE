{{Template:Knight navigation bar}} The way to recycle technotrash through TechnoCycle is to mail it to "Recycling, Building NW62" via interdepartmental mail. If your technotrash doesn't fit in an interdepartmental envelope, it's almost certainly inappropriate for TechnoCycle, which is for batteries, media, hard drives, and portable electronics like cellphones. If you have a (deactivate) display, computer, or larger item, consider posting it to reuse@mit.edu or see the instructions for recycling here: http://web.mit.edu/facilities/environmental/reuse.html
