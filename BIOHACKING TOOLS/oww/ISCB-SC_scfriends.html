{{ISCB-SC}} == Mailing list == The ISCB-SC has created a new mailing list to inform non-ISCB members about Student Council activities. Anyone will be able to sign up. Only important announcements will be sent to the list, to keep the traffic on the list low. You can also a list of recent postings at: http://iscbsc.org/pipermail/scfriends_iscbsc.org/ == Sign up == Anyone can sign up at: http://iscbsc.org/mailman/listinfo/scfriends_iscbsc.org You can sign yourself off the list at any time you like.
