The main goal of Screening Plasmid 2.0 is to enable performance similar to [[Endy:Screening plasmid 1.0|SP1.0]], but at a lower copy (either 3 or 4 series plasmids from the [[Registry]].) Additionally, we would like to be able to move the SP into a wider range of strains.
