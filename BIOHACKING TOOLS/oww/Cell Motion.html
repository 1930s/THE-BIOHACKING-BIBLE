==Cell Velocity Measurements== I've been trying to figure out statistics on cell motion. Using ImarisXT combined with MATLAB - I can get all sorts of statistics out of cell tracking. I use Spots and Spot Tracking in Imaris to track the cells - then take the numbers into MATLAB and analyze them. I have found that the cell statistics seem to follow a Poisson distribution. I think it's due to the discrete nature of cell binding events, or possibly protrusion events. ---- Back to [[User:Timp0 | Winston]]
