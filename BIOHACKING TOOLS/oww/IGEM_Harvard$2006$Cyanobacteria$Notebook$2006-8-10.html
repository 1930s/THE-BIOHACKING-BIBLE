==Ligation Attempt #2 of J04450+PSB4A3 (see Monday)== Previous ligation attempt did not survive the resistance innoculation. Due to the low amounts of the ligation product, will redo experiments but allign molar amounts as per Endy protocol instead of using a fixed amount like last time: Although the Silver protocol recommends using 50-100ng BB vector + 4-10x insert molar mass, Endy recommends a 3-fold molar excess and less DNA. Roche kit says 1:1 or 1:2 works for DNA which is not similiar in length. Seeing as how our total vector + insert DNA is very low concentration, we will use a ''' 1:2 '''ratio. '''Ligation'''
*BB vector pSB4A3: 50ng @ BB backbone high copy 1 (3.2 ng/uL) = 15.625 uL *BB insert J04450: 2*(~1069bp/3339bp)*50ng=24.01 ng @ (6.0 ng/uL) = 5.33 uL *1x DNA Dilution Buffer: 4 uL -------- Protocol (''for this only because it is above 10uL''): *Mix above ratios + 25uL #1 + 2.5uL #3 **BE sure to mix #1 (ligation buffer) well especially! *Sit at bench for ~30min *Put back on ice '''Transformation''' For the transformation I did 6 samples: *1 exp. Top10 (50uL), Positive control, negative control (25uL ea.) *1 exp. Top10F (50uL), Positive control, negative control (25uL ea.) Ice incubation 30m, 30s@42C, 2m@ice, SOC@1h@37C. Plated on LBCarb.
