lactone rings from the acyl moieties of AHLs and renders the AHLs inactive in signal transduction (Dong et al. 2000). AiiA is extremely nonspecific with regard to the AHL acyl side chain, which suggests that this strategy interferes generically with AHL-mediated communication between gram-positive bacteria (Dong et al. 2000) ''Bacillus'' relies on oligopeptide-mediated quorum sensing. Therefore, this tactic, while disrupting gram-negative bacterial communication, leaves ''Bacillus'' cell-cell communication unperturbed." (Quorum Sensing: Cell-to-Cell Communication in Bacteria) - ''Christopher M. Waters and Bonnie L. Bassler'' (Annu. Rev. Cell Dev.
