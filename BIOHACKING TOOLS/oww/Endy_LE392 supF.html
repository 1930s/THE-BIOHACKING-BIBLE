Back to [[Endy:E._coli_Strains| Endy lab E coli strains]] * '''Strain:''' LE392 supF * '''Genotype:''' * '''Phenotype/Notes:''' Amber suppressor strain * '''Date:''' 12/2005 * '''Experimenter:''' [[User:Stpierre|Francois St-Pierre]] * '''Strain obtained from:''' Generous gift from Prof. Amos Oppenheim. * '''Stock preparation:''' The strain was received as an agar plate. Since no single colonies were obtained, a new plate was streaked and incubated at 37C. A single colony was grown in LB at 30C for about 10 hours. Glycerol stocks were made by mixing 1ml of culture with 1ml of 40% glycerol, vortexing, and storing at -85C. * '''Physical location of the stock:''' ** Lab stocks: none ** [[User:Stpierre|Francois St-Pierre]]'s stocks: 2 vials in box "Francois's Ecoli 1" (as of 12/2005)
