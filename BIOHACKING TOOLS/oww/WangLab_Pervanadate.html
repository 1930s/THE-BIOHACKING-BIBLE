=== Time to prepare === 10 minutes === Ingredients === * H2O * HEPES, 20 mM * Na3VO4 (in freezer) * Catalase (in freezer) * Hydrogen Peroxide, or H2O2, 30%. === Preparation of pervanadate (Huyer G et al., JBC 1997) === # Take one tube of 100mM sodium orthovanadate Na3VO4 (Must be white/colorless. If it shows yellow, means VO4 forms 12mer, cannot use.) # Prepare 20mM HEPES adjust PH to 7.3 # Take 30% H2O2 dilute 10 times with HEPES buffer, 5 ul 30% H2O2 + 45 ul HEPES buffer, mix gently. # Dilute 3% H2O2 10 times further with HEPES buffer, 5 ul 3% H2O2 + 45 ul HEPES buffer, mix gently. # Add in 10ul 100mM Na3VO4, 940ul H2O. Mix by gently reversing the vial. This will make up a 1mM pervanadate stock. # After 5 minutes, use the tip of pipet to scoop a small amount of catalase, mix in to the pervanadate stock. This will result in a burst of O2 bubbles. Open the lid of the tube to release the air pressure. # The pervanadate is good for several hours (<24 hours).
