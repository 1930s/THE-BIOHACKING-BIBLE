==Motivations== *With this testing part, we want to characterize the sensitivity of the prey cell towards the prey molecule (AHL) *By testing the part firstly with known AHL concentrations, a transfer curve can be established *By referring to the transfer function, this part will serve as an assay for AHL concentrations, which allows us to quantize different AHL concentrations *The expression of GFP and thus the level of fluorescence is dependent on the concentration of AHL ==Protocol== See [[IGEM:IMPERIAL/Protocols/T9002|T9002 Testing Protocol]] ==Results== Testing Results (processed data) for T9002: *[http://openwetware.org/images/6/62/020906_T9002_processed.xls 02/09/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/1/1f/010906_T9002_processed.xls 01/09/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/b/b7/310806_T9002_processed.xls 31/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/f/fd/300806_T9002_processed.xls 30/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/3/39/280806_T9002_processed.xls 28/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/6/65/270806_T9002_processed.xls 27/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/a/af/250806_T9002_processed.xls 25/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/8/8a/240806_T9002_Data_Spreadsheet.xls 24/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/3/34/Copy_of_T9002_Data_Spreadsheet_2308_2.xls 23/08/2006] *[http://openwetware.org/images/8/82/Copy_of_T9002_Data_Spreadsheet2.xls 22/08/2006 plate 1] and [http://openwetware.org/images/2/2f/Copy_of_T9002_Data_Spreadsheet3.xls 22/08/2006 plate 2] (96-well plate was left in shaker overnight) ==Parameter extraction== *Demonstrate how you are extracting the paramters you are interested in from experimental data. *Give the results.
