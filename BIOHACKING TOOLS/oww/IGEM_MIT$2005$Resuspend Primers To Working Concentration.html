===Resuspend Primers=== Centrifuge vial 1 min. to allow "dry" DNA to fall to bottom. Resuspend primers to working concentration. [[Image:Conversions.xls|PrimerResuspensionConversion.xls]] Find nmoles on order form for primer. Convert nmoles to 20 picomoles/uL to find X. Pipette X uL 1X TE into vial. Vortex 1 min. Insert BLUE label-cap. Label appropriately. Store bottom shelf -20 Freezer in PRIMER box.
