=BG-11 liquid media= *Cyanobacteria, including Synechocystis PCC 6803, are typically grown in BG-11 media. *Obtain 50x BG-11 media from Sigma-Aldrich (catalog # C3061-500ML). *In the biosafety cabinet, mix 20 mL 50x BG-11 media and 980 mL Millipore water. *Autoclave for 15 minutes at 121C in 500 mL bottles to sterilize. *Contents of 1x liquid media: **NaNO3 1.5 g/L **K2HPO4 0.04 g/L **MgSO4.7H2O 0.075 g/L **CaCl2.2H2O 0.036 g/L **Citric acid 6.0 mg/L **EDTA 1.0 mg/L **Na2CO3 0.02 g/L **Ferric ammonium citrate 6.0 mg/L **H3BO3 2.86 mg/L **MnCl2.4H2O 1.81 mg/L **ZnSO4.7H2O 0.222 mg/L **Na2MoO4.2H2O 0.39 mg/L **CuSO4.5H2O 0.079 mg/L **Co(NO3)2.6H2O 0.0494 mg/L **Distilled water 1.0 L **sources: Sigma-Aldrich & ATCC (media 616) =BG-11 plates= *For 1 L, or ~2 sleeves of plates, place in a 2L flask: **10 mL 1 M TES pH 8.2 (Sigma catalog #) **3 g sodium thiosulfate (Sigma, catalog #) **15 g Bacto-agar (catalog #) **20 mL 50x BG-11 media (Sigma catalog #) **980 mL distilled water **a stirring bar *Autoclave at 121C for 20 minutes on the liquid cycle. *Allow to cool, with stirring, until ~50C or cool enough to handle.
