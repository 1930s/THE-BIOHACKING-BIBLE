[[Image:userpic.png|350px|thumb|right|Photo courtesy of Dirk Neumann]] =About= This page is supposed to contain only a brief summary about me. =Research Interests= ===Goal=== My general goal is to combine computational modeling and experimental tools to build a general framework for understanding initiation of signaling events, leading to specific cellular responses. My current research focus is on understanding the coupling between the [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T_cell_receptor T cell receptor] (TCR) signaling -- the essential signal for T cell activation -- and the [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Co-stimulation costimulatory signalings] -- the auxillary signals thought to affect T cell activation by modulating TCR signaling. Other areas of research I would like to explore include the signaling cascades involved in [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T_cell T cell]/[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Antigen_presenting_cell Antigen presenting cell] interactions, in [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Circadian_rhythm circadian rhythm], in [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cell_migration cell migration] and those in [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mechanotransduction mechanotransduction]. ===Past Works=== * A Kinetic modeling of nucleosome unwrapping * Testing population level variability of tet-controlled gene expression system using pCLPIT * Analysis of pCLPIT plasmid construct as a tet-controlled gene expression system * ELISA method development for E-cadherin/beta-catenin complex detection =Biosketch= ===Massachusetts Institute of Technology=== * 2006 - ** I am a first year graduate student in [http://web.mit.edu/be/ biological engineering] at [http://www.mit.edu MIT] and currently a rotation student in the Chakraborty lab. ===California Institute of Technology=== * 2002 - 2006 ** I was an undergraduate student at [http://www.caltech.edu Caltech], studying biology and engineering & applied science (with coursework emphasis in bioengineering (BE) and computation & neural system(CNS)). ===The Taft School=== * 2001 - 2002 ** I attended [http://www.taftschool.org Taft School] as a post-graduate high school student, preparing for US colleges. ===Siriraj Medical College=== *2000 - 2001 ** I was a first year medical student at the Siriraj medical school, Mahidol University before I decided to change my path to do study abroad in science. =Links= * [[BE Board]] * [[Immunology Papers]]
