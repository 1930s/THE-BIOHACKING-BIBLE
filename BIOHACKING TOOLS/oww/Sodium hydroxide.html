===Sodium hydroxide=== * A strong base * Stock solutions are 5 N and 1 N * Molecular weight 40.00 * Exothermic reaction on solution * Stock solutions should be stored in plastic bottles, rather than glass. ** Glass bottles are etched by strong bases over long periods of time. * Stock solutions absorb carbon dioxide from the air, and gradually become neutralized. **Check normality every year, or remake solutions. * Very caustic, wear gloves during use. Wash extensively if spilled. A soap-like feel indicates that your skin has come in contact with the solution. * Neutralize with acid prior to disposal down the drain.
