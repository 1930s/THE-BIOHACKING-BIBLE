==Purpose/Procedure== The purpose of this lab was to discover the "spin factor", gs, of an electron buy finding its resonance frequencies in a magnetic field. We excite electrons with a Radio Frequency generator and place them in an external magnetic field. Observing the energy levels with an oscilloscope, when we see a dip in energies, thats where the electron has aligned itself with the field (thus observing the spin orientation) For a detailed procedure and pictures of the setup please see my lab notebook [http://openwetware.org/wiki/Physics307L:People/Le/Notebook/071105#Procedure] ==Data== We measured the frequency of the RF generator and the current going through the Helmholtz Coils to find the magnetic field.
