* [http://www.broad.mit.edu/wga/ Arachne], [http://genome.cs.mtu.edu/cap/cap3.html CAP3] and [http://www.phrap.org/ Phrap] are some well known assembly programs. You have to extract the traces manually though. * [http://genotrace.niob.knaw.nl Genotrace] ([http://genotrace.niob.knaw.nl/1.0/ source]) is a program for "local assembly" (you could also call it "partial assembly") which assembles only those parts from the genome that you're interested in.
