=='''Preparing Complete Media:'''== Obtain 500 ml bottle of media. Add 5.5 ml L-glutamine (1/100). Add 2.75 ml Pen Strep (1/200). Add 50 ml FCS for 10% mixture; or add 75 ml for 15% mixture. =='''Preparing 293 GPG Media:'''== Obtain 500 ml bottle of DMEM. Add 5 ml Pen Strep :50 ml FCS :112 ul Tet (5 mg/ml) :560 ul Puro (2 mg/ml) :1.7 ml G418 (400 mg/ml) =='''Thawing Cells:'''== 1. Remove from liquid nitrogen tank. 2. Defrost in 37 degree water bath. 3. Add cells to 10 ml incomplete media. 4. Spin down for 5 min. at 2000 rpm’s. 5. Decant liquid. 6. Add 5 ml of complete media; mix well. 7. Transfer to flask/plate. 8. Incubate. =='''Trypsinizing Cells:'''== 1. Remove media. 2. Wash with PBS 1X. 3. Add trypsin (~3 ml for large flask). 4. Incubate 1 to 2 min. (Don't leave any longer than necessary!!!) 5. Tap flask if needed to loosen cells. 6. Add media; mix. 7. Transfer to new container. =='''Harvesting Cells:'''== 1. Discard media. 2. Add PBS (~5 ml for a large plate). 3. Scrape cells loose. 4. Pipet into tube. 5. Spin 5 min. at 2000 rpm’s. 6. Decant liquid. 7. Add 5 ml PBS to tube; resuspend cells. 8. Transfer to round bottom (14 ml) tube if necessary. 9. Spin again; decant liquid. 10. Cap tightly and place in -20 freezer. [[Kupfer Lab:Back Door|Return to protocols]]
