For photographs of our specific setup. ==Purpose== {{DH comment|label=2|comment=Maybe cite where you got that number}} The purpose of the Millikan oil drop experiment is to measure the charge of an electron. This value is found by balancing the electric and gravitational forces on tiny oil drops sprayed in between two charged plates. By measuring the travel speeds of the oil drops both in an electric field and when left only to gravity, one should find values that are always integer multiples of a given amount. This amount is the charge of an electron (1.602 x 10^-19 C).
