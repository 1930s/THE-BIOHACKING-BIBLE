However, for biomedical use it is considered to be an irritant (label Xi R36/37/38). Nitrile gloves of 0.11mm thickness ensures protection. Because of its intercalating nature it is disposed of with dangerous substances aside from carcinogens. (Wear gloves and consider disposal in the same way you dispose of ethidium bromide). French: Le dichlorhydrate de Diamidino-4',6-phénylindole-2 ou DAPI n'est pas classé comme substance dangereuse en Europe au sens de la Directive 67/548.
