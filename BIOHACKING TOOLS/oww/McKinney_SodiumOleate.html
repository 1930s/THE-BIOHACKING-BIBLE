===Sodium oleate solution=== This is an additive to media used to culture ''M. tuberculosis.'' ===Ingredients=== (make 500mL) * 12.5mL 2M NaOH * 6.0mL oleic acid (=5g, since oleic acid specific gravity is 1g/1.2mL) * 481.5mL water ===Protocol=== Combine the above ingredients in a 1L flask. Stir with a magnetic stirbar until completely dissolved. Filter sterilize through a 0.22μm pore filter. Store at room temperature wrapped in aluminum foil (oleic acid is light sensitive).
