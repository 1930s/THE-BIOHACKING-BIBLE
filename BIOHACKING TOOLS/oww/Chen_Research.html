Research Overview

Our research interests are to understand cellular and molecular mechanisms underlying the development and function of the immune system. We specifically study: * [[Chen:Antigen receptor gene assembly | Control of antigen receptor gene assembly and dysregulation of the process in lymphoid tumorigenesis.]] * [[Chen:Immunological memory | Cellular and molecular basis of immunological memory, focusing on CD8 T cell responses to virus infection in the lung and prostate cancer. ]] * [[Chen:Prophylaxis and therapies against influenza | Novel prophylaxis and therapies against virus infection in the lung. ]]
