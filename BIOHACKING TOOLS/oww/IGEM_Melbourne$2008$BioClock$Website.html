[[IGEM:Melbourne/2008|Return to Melbourne Homepage]] [[IGEM:Melbourne/2008/BioClock|Return to Bioclock]] I propose 2 ways to proceed. One is to learn from evolutionary history of biological systems on how biological clock is made. This approach has the advantage of learning what sort of biochemistry may be required for building a clock.
