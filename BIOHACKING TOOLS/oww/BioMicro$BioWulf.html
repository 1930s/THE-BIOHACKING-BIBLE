[http://biowulf.mit.edu Biowulf]: A 32 node compute cluster comprised of dual [http://www.amd.com/us-en/ AMD] Athlon CPUs. This cluster was purchase from [http://www.microway.com Microway]. While biowulf is setup in a similar fashion to RoadRunner, we are running the MauiScheduler rather than the [CScheduler] (default for [OpenPBS]).
