{{Griffitts}} ==N3 Phage Transduction (''S. meliloti'')== ===Preparation of lysate=== * Set up 24-hr [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB]] culture of each donor strain, so it reaches OD600=~1.0 * Dilute Phage stock appropriately (10-4 if standard lysate) in [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB-Ca]]—be sure to add the [[Griffitts:Stock solutions#1 M Calcium Chloride (75 mL)|calcium]]! * Add [[Griffitts:Stock solutions#1 M Calcium Chloride (75 mL)|1 M CaCl2]] to each culture, to 3 mM * Mix 20 μL of the phage dilution with 400 μL bacterial culture in a 15-mL tube * Incubate at 30°C for 30 min * Add 10 mL of 42°C [[Griffitts:Common media#LB-Top agar (500 mL)|LB-Ca top agar]]—be sure to add the [[Griffitts:Stock solutions#1 M Calcium Chloride (75 mL)|calcium]]! * Dump contents into an empty petri plate * Incubate at 30°C for 15-18 h ===Phage Harvesting=== * Add 2 mL [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB-Ca]]—be sure to add the [[Griffitts:Stock solutions#1 M Calcium Chloride (75 mL)|calcium]]! * Mash the top agar for 2 min with spreader * Recover 1 mL to microfuge tube * Spin down debris * Move 800 μL to a new microfuge tube * Add 50 μL CHCl3, shake, spin * Store at 4°C * This stock is about 1010 PFU/mL. ===Transduction=== * Grow each recipient strain to OD600=1.0 (109 CFU/mL) in [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB]] * Add [[Griffitts:Stock solutions#1 M Calcium Chloride (75 mL)|1 M CaCl2]] to each culture, to 3 mM * Combine 30 μL or 90 μL of lysate with 1 mL of culture (one may work better) * Incubate at 30°C for 30 min * Centrifuge and resuspend in [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB]] (no calcium) 3 times * Centrifuge and resuspend in 70 μL of [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB]] * Plate on selective medium, [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB]] (no calcium) ==M12 Phage Transduction (''S. meliloti'')== ==P1 Phage Transduction (''E. coli'')== ===Preparation of lysate=== * Set up 24-hr [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB]] culture of each donor strain, so it reaches OD600=~1.0 * Dilute Phage stock appropriately (10-2 or even 10-1 if standard lysate) in [[Griffitts:Common media#LB Broth (1 L)|LB-Ca]]—be sure to add the [[Griffitts:Stock solutions#1 M Calcium Chloride (75 mL)|calcium]]!
