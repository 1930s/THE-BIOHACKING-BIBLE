=BioBrick™ Standard License v0.2= The BioBricks™ Foundation is working to develop a framework that can be used to define the means by which BioBrick™ standard biological parts can be shared, distributed, modified, and used. Our goal is to provide the designers and users of BioBrick™ standard biological parts with zero transaction cost ''options'' for controlling the terms by which BioBrick™ standard biological parts are developed and deployed.

The BioBrick™ Standard License v0.2 (aka, BSL v0.2) is based on options. And if you wait just a minute you'll get to see them and provide comments!
