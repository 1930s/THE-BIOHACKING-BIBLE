==Mode of Action== Inhibits protein synthesis; binds to 30S ribosomal subunit. The rpsL50 gene mutation conveys streptomycin resistance. Resistance is recessive, so the presence of a wild type rpsL gene will convey sensitivity. This allows counter-selection against the presence of the wt rpsL gene. Mutations of wild type genes to convey resistance are common. ==Mechanism of Resistance== ==Usage Notes== #100 mg/mL stock solution in H2O.
