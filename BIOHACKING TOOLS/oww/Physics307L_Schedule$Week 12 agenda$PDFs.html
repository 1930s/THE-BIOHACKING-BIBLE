===Lots of algorithms available for generating random numbers from 0 to 1 with uniform probability=== ===General case=== ====Assume you have a random number r from 0 to 1. To transform to x, with PDF given by P(x):==== :r=\int_{X=-\infty}^{x} P(x)\, dx ====Numerically solving for x gives random number with appropriate distribution.==== ===Specific case, time for random event to occur (i.e. radioactive decay)=== :P(t) = \frac {1}{\tau} e^{-\frac{t}{\tau}} ====Solving above,==== :\ t_i = -\tau \ln r_i
