All sequences on this page are in GenBank format. * [[Media:jg1100.gb|'''JG1100''']]: stably replicating plasmid in ''E. coli''; suicide plasmid in mycobacteria (must recombine into the genome to perpetuate). Can transform at least ''M. tuberculosis'' and ''M. smegmatis.'' Encodes ampicilin, hygromycin, and kanamycin resistance. Plasmid, sequence, and annotations obtained from Neeraj Dhar.
