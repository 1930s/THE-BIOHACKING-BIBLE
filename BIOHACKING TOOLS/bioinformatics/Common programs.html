Everyday bioinformatics is done with sequence search programs like [http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/blast/index.html BLAST], sequence analysis programs, like the [http://www.emboss.org/ EMBOSS] and [http://www.mrc-lmb.cam.ac.uk/pubseq/ Staden] packages, structure prediction programs like [http://bioinf.cs.ucl.ac.uk/threader/threader.html THREADER] or [http://maple.bioc.columbia.edu/predictprotein/ PHD] or molecular imaging/modelling programs like [http://www.inpharmatica.co.uk/rasmol/ RasMol] and [http://www.cmbi.kun.nl/whatif/ WHATIF].
