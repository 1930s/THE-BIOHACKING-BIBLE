===Introduction=== Hello. My name is Damian Counsell. Until August 2005 I was a [http://bioinformatics.org/faq/#definitions Bioinformatics] Specialist in the United Kingdom [http://www.mrc.ac.uk/ Medical Research Council]'s [http://www.rfcgr.mrc.ac.uk/ Rosalind Franklin Centre for Genomics Research]. I am still a director of Bioinformatics.Org, on the Advisory Panel of [http://www.touchbriefings.com/cdps/cditem.cfm?NID=1619 Future Drug Discovery], but I am not a scientist.
